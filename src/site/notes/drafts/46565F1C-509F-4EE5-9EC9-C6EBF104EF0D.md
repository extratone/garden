## Noun

1. an imaginary monster used to frighten children
	- Synonyms
		- bogeyman
		- bugbear
		- boogeyman
		- booger
	- Less specific
		- monster
2. a source of concern; “the old bugaboo of inflation still bothers them”
	- Less specific
		- concern
		- worry
		- headache
		- vexation
