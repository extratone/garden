## Retrieve the raw contents of a Discourse forum thread in markdown.

Crafted [with the help of gluebyte](https://reddit.com/r/shortcuts/comments/t21vfe/get_raw_version_of_discourse_thread/), this shortcut converts a given Discourse forum thread link to its raw equivalent, downloads the contents at that destination, and copies the text to the clipboard.
