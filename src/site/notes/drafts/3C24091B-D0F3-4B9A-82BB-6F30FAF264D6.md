## Noun

1. a woman’s hairstyle in which the hair gives a puffy appearance
	- Less specific
		- hairdo
		- hairstyle
		- hair style
		- coiffure
		- coif

## Adjective

	- Synonyms
		- puffy
	- Similar to
		- large
		- big
