# sidereal


## Adjective

1. of or relating to the stars or constellations; “sidereal bodies”; “the sidereal system”
	- Pertains to
		- constellation
2. (of divisions of time) determined by daily motion of the stars; “sidereal time”
	- Antonyms
		- civil
