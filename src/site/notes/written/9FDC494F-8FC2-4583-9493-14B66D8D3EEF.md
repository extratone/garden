# somnolent


## Adjective

1. inclined to or marked by drowsiness; “slumberous (or slumbrous) eyes”; “`slumbery’ is archaic”; “the sound had a somnolent effect”
	- Synonyms
		- slumberous
		- slumbery
		- slumbrous
	- Similar to
		- asleep
	- Related
		- sleepiness
		- drowsiness
		- somnolence
		- sleep
		- slumber
