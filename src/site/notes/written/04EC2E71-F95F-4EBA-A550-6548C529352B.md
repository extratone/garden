# slipshod


## Adjective

1. marked by great carelessness; “a most haphazard system of record keeping”; “slapdash work”; “slipshod spelling”; “sloppy workmanship”
	- Synonyms
		- haphazard
		- slapdash
		- sloppy
	- Similar to
		- careless
	- Related
		- carelessness
		- sloppiness
